Stockwatch subscription service

Jun 2017

Based on the results of a stock-related research project, I wrote a business plan for an information subscription service which uses the novel algorithm developed to help day traders become more profitable.

Stockwatch is the envisioned product, a web application offering subscribed users a dashboard of information generated through the research. In addition to signals of buy or sell resulting from directional price predictions, users also benefit from an analysis of opinion shift, real-time event detection and alerts, and the tailoring of algorithms to their personal portfolios.

Along with the business plan, a prototype was built and pitched to an industry panel, with good feedback received.
