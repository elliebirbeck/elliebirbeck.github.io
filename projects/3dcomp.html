PROJECT : 3D Computer Graphics

In December 2016 I created a short animation for an Animation Production unit as part of my degree. This process involved the complete 3D CG production pipeline of storyboarding, modelling, texturing, UV mapping, rigging, animating, lighting, and rendering. I also had some experience with dynamics and MEL scripting. The software used for this project was Maya, and Adobe Premiere Pro for post-production.



In November 2015 I created several pieces of work for a 3D Computer Imaging unit whilst I was studying abroad. Through this I developed skills in modelling, texturing, UV mapping, lighting, compositing, and rendering. The software used was Blender.
